toecaps
which feed
formations into
the dancefloor

inventory
figures
in balloon silk
and open flowing jackets

the ascent
in any event
imperative

you just duck away
under the side of the hand
eyebrow, salute

applause beforehand
head to head

to reach into the jacket pocket
scares the phone from its burrow

a thread
from the labyrinth-
thesaurus
